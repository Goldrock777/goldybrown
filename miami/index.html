Luxury Leather Bags, Delivered to Miami

Goldy Brown ships full grain calfskin holsters, travel bags, and bespoke commissions free of charge across Miami and South Florida. Designed in Vancouver, made in South India by master artisans, delivered to Miami Beach, Brickell, and Bal Harbour in three to five business days via DHL or FedEx.

Built for the boat day, the gala, and the redeye to LaGuardia

The Holster is a body-worn travel bag — the answer to the Miami client's daily problem of moving between marina, dinner, gallery opening, and Friday flight without a third bag. Pouch fits passport, phone, AirPods, sunglasses, and a slim wallet. Worn under a tropical wool blazer at the Faena, over a linen shirt at Joia, or over a Brunello tee on the boat, it disappears.

Clients across South Beach, Mid-Beach, Sunset Islands, Star and Palm, Brickell, Coconut Grove, Coral Gables, and Bal Harbour use theirs daily. The full grain calfskin handles humidity well; we recommend conditioning twice a year if you live near the water.
