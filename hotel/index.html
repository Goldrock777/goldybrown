Why hotels work with Goldy Brown

Most hotel guest amenity kits are sourced from the same handful of large suppliers, finished in the same handful of colourways, and arrive in the same handful of presentation boxes. The Goldy Brown Hotel Programme exists for properties whose brand demands more — a kit that reads as a piece of the property itself, not a logo'd freebie.

We design and manufacture in-house, from a Vancouver atelier and a network of master leather artisans in South India whose tannery houses have supplied European luxury for over a century. Every order is bespoke. The leather is cut to your selection, dyed to your colourway, hot-stamped or hand-tattooed with your crest, and presented in packaging built for your room.

Frequently asked questions

What is the minimum order?

Programme One starts at $10,000 USD and produces approximately 100 to 200 finished pieces depending on configuration. Volume discounts begin at 250 pieces.

How long from brief to delivery?

Six to ten weeks. Two weeks for design and physical samples, six to eight weeks for production and air-freight shipping. Rush programmes for property openings can be quoted at four weeks.

Are samples available?

Yes. We send a physical sample kit to your property at no cost on serious enquiries. The sample includes a stitched holster, a passport sleeve, a card holder, and a luggage tag in your selected leather.

Can leather be matched to our property colours?

Yes. We dye to specification using vegetable or chrome tanning. Standard palettes include cognac, oxblood, midnight, forest, and ivory. Bespoke colours add one week to the timeline.

What about repair and warranty?

Hotel Programme pieces are backed by the same lifetime warranty as our retail goods — any defect in stitching, leather, or hardware is repaired or replaced at our atelier with shipping covered both ways.

Can we re-order at the same per-unit cost?

Yes. Once a programme is established, the bill of materials is locked and re-orders ship at the original per-unit price for 24 months.
