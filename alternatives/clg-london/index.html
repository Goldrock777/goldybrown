Goldy Brown vs CLG London

CLG London is the leather goods house most often compared to Goldy Brown for the hotel guest amenity programme work. Both houses make full grain leather kits for 4- and 5-star properties. Here is the honest comparison if you are choosing between us.

Up front CLG London is a respected, well-established maker. Their published roster includes Claridge's, the Dorchester, the Connaught, the Berkeley, Raffles London, Hilton, and Four Seasons Philadelphia. We are a younger, smaller atelier. The right choice between us is a function of your brief. This page tries to help you choose, not to sell against them.

Where Goldy Brown wins on the brief

  • Published tier pricing. Our $10K / $35K / $100K Hotel Programme tiers are public on /hotel/. Procurement teams can run a budget conversation in 30 seconds without a discovery call.
  • Origin transparency. We're explicit about where the leather is tanned (South India) and where the workshops sit. Some clients want that paper trail in a sustainability or supplier-diversity audit.
  • Lifetime warranty without paperwork. No proof of purchase chases. The warranty card is handwritten and ships with every order — guests reach out directly years later.
  • Hand-tattooed personalisation. Freehand crests, signatures, and sketches etched into the front face — a depth of personalisation that hot-foil cannot match. Useful for milestone gifting.
  • Vancouver design + global shipping. Designed in BC, shipped DHL/FedEx/Canada Post free worldwide. Useful for North American property groups doing a global rollout.

Where CLG London is the better choice

  • If the property has been a CLG client for five-plus years and the existing programme is working — switching makers for a stable programme is rarely worth the disruption.
  • If a London-based, in-person review of every batch is part of your QC requirement, CLG's London proximity to UK and EU 5-star clients is a real advantage.
  • If the client brief specifically requires "Made in Britain" labelling — CLG's UK-and-EU production is a closer fit. Our pieces are designed in Vancouver and made in South India and we don't carry a "Made in Britain" mark.
  • If brand reference matters more than the brief. If your GM or commercial director has worked with CLG before and the relationship is the comfort, that's a legitimate reason to stay.
The right maker for a property is not necessarily the one with the longest hotel client list. It's the one whose lead time, leather, personalisation depth, and economics line up with your specific kit.

If you are running a procurement RFP, we are happy to be quoted alongside CLG. We will provide a sample kit at no cost on a serious enquiry. We will also tell you, in writing, where we think CLG is the better fit and where we are. The decision is yours.

The honest summary

If you want a London-based, established maker with a long 5-star client roster and you have the budget, CLG London is a safe choice.

If you want published tier pricing, origin transparency, hand-tattooed personalisation, a lifetime warranty without paperwork, and per-unit costs roughly 25–40% lower at comparable volumes — and you are open to a younger atelier with a tightly edited line — we'd love to send you a sample kit.
