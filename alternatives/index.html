How we write these comparisons

Three rules:

  • No disparagement. If a competitor is better at something, we say so.
  • Verifiable facts only. Pricing tiers, lead times, leather origin, materials, warranty terms. No claims we cannot back up.
  • Honest positioning. If you are choosing between Goldy Brown and another house, the right house depends on your brief. We will tell you when the other house is the better fit.

If your house is being compared to ours and you'd like a correction, please email press@goldybrown.com. We update facts within 48 hours.
