# Email Sequences — Goldy Brown

Implements the `email-sequence` skill. Four sequences, written in brand voice, ready to load into Klaviyo / Mailchimp / Customer.io.

Three rules across all sequences:

1. **No subject-line tricks.** "Re:" or "fw:" or fake-personal subjects break trust. Subjects should describe what's inside.
2. **Send from a real person**, not `noreply@`. `info@goldybrown.com` is fine; a founder name is better for the high-touch sequences.
3. **Plain text whenever possible.** Image-heavy emails go to Promotions tab. The brand's voice survives plain text well.

> · Care reminder: condition once or twice a year if you haven't already
>

>
> — Goldy Brown

### Email 4.2 — 14 days after 4.1, only if 4.1 was opened but not clicked

**Subject:** Honest question

> One question, no setup:
>
> What would have to be true for you to buy from us a second time?
>
> Reply and tell us. Even a one-line answer is useful. We read every email at the bench.
>
> — Goldy Brown

If they don't open 4.1 OR 4.2, drop them from the list. Don't keep emailing.

## Implementation notes

### Where these go
Load into the email tool of choice. Recommended: Klaviyo (best for Shopify), Customer.io, ConvertKit, or Mailchimp. Avoid Constant Contact (the platform is ageing).

### Personalisation tokens
- `{first_name}` — fallback to "there" or remove the greeting if not available
- `{order_summary}` — list of items + total
- `{cart_link}` — preserved cart URL with token
- `{tracking_number}` — from carrier API
- `{referral_link}` — generated per-customer (see REFERRAL-PROGRAM.md)

### Plain text vs HTML
All four sequences should send as plain text by default — the brand reads better in monospace than in heavy HTML templates. Add light HTML only for tracking/links if your platform requires it.

### Send-time optimisation
- Welcome 1.1: send-on-trigger (immediate)
- Abandoned 2.1: 60 minutes
- Post-purchase 3.x: send-on-trigger or scheduled
- Win-back: scheduled at 9am local to recipient (most platforms can do this)

### Suppression
- Don't send abandoned-cart to subscribers who later complete the purchase
- Don't send win-back to customers who've ordered in the last 12 months
- Honour every unsubscribe within 24 hours
