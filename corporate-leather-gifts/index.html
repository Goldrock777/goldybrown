Branding done right

The most common mistake in corporate gifting is over-branding. A leather card holder with a 4cm company logo on the front reads as a tradeshow giveaway and ends up in a drawer. A leather card holder with the logo discreetly stamped on the inside flap, the recipient's initials hot-stamped on the corner, reads as a personal gift the company chose for them.

What we recommend:

  • Logo on the inside, not the outside
  • Recipient's initials, not their full name
  • Hot-stamp in foil that complements the leather (gold on cognac, silver on black, blind-emboss for the most restrained version)
  • Linen presentation envelope or box, with a wax seal and a handwritten card from the gifting executive
