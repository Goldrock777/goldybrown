Luxury Leather Goods, Designed in Vancouver

Goldy Brown is a Vancouver-based leather atelier producing full grain calfskin holsters, travel bags, passport holders, card holders, and luggage tags for clients across Canada and the world. Every piece is designed in our Vancouver studio and crafted in South India by master artisans whose tannery houses have supplied European luxury for over a century.

A Vancouver atelier, a global supply chain

The brand was founded in Vancouver because the city sits at a particular intersection — Pacific weather that rewards weatherproof goods, a discerning customer base accustomed to travel, and a small-batch maker culture that prizes craft over volume. Our designs respond to all three. The Holster, our signature piece, was prototyped on the SkyTrain between YVR and downtown after one too many trips through security with a pile of pockets.

We don't run a retail storefront. The atelier is a working studio — appointment-only — where commissions are sketched, leather is cut, and finished pieces are inspected before shipping. If you'd like to visit, email info@goldybrown.com.

The Vancouver lifestyle context

Vancouver clients tend to travel often and pack lightly. The Holster was built for that — a single piece that holds passport, phone, AirPods, and a slim wallet, worn under a jacket so airline staff treat it as a worn accessory rather than a third bag. We've tested it through YVR's NEXUS lanes, the AirTrain at JFK, and Heathrow Terminal 5; the stainless steel hardware is the same kind found in any belt or watch and passes scanners without incident.

For local use, the holster sits well over a Mackage shell or under a Filson waxed jacket. The leather darkens with West Coast rain and develops a real patina inside the first six months.

Lifetime warranty, repaired locally

Every piece is backed by the Goldy Brown Guarantee — any defect in stitching, leather, or hardware is repaired or replaced at the Vancouver atelier for life. Shipping is covered both ways. We don't ask for a receipt. A handwritten guarantee card ships with every order.
