Branded Leather for Private Clubs

Custom-branded full grain leather for golf, country, tennis, racquet, yacht, and city clubs. Member welcome kits, tournament gifts, pro shop merchandise, member milestone pieces. Club crest hand-tattooed or hot-stamped, leather dyed to club colours.

Where the leather lives in club life

New member welcome kit

The first physical brand touch a member has. A passport sleeve, card holder, and luggage tag — club crest hot-stamped, member's initials inside.

Tournament gifts

The major member-guest, club championship, or invitational. Branded leather scorecard holders, yardage book covers, ball pouches, divot tools.

Pro shop merchandise

Retail-tier kit available year-round in the pro shop or online. Club crest, retail margin, no per-piece personalisation required.

Member milestone

The 25-year, 50-year, life-member gift. Hand-tattooed family crest piece — a holster, briefcase, or document folio reserved for senior members.

Hole-in-one and trophy gifts

The leather presentation case for a hole-in-one ball, championship trophy, or member-guest plate. Hand-tooled, club-engraved.

Pro and staff gifting

Year-end gifts for the head professional, the GM, the agronomy team. The opposite of generic — hand-tattooed with their initials.

Visiting professional kits

For invitational tournaments where touring pros visit. Branded amenity kit in the locker — passport sleeve, card holder, tag.

Annual member dinner gift

The piece that arrives at the place setting at the annual member dinner. Card holder or luggage tag, club crest, member's initials.

Club crests, done right. We hand-tattoo club arms and crests freehand into the front face of the leather — the design is etched in, not foil-stamped on top. The depth is the same as the leather grain itself, and it ages with the piece. For pro shop merchandise we use hot-stamping in metallic foil; for member milestone pieces we hand-tattoo. Both are included in the per-unit pricing above.

Two programme types

The Member Programme

For onboarding new members, milestone gifting, and the annual member-event piece. Typical batch is 50 to 250 pieces. Lead time is 6 to 8 weeks. The leather is dyed to club colours (we work most often in cognac, oxblood, forest, and navy). The club crest is the constant; per-member personalisation (initials) is optional.

The Tournament Programme

For invitational events, member-guest weekends, club championships, and signature pro-am tournaments. Typical batch is 60 to 200 pieces (the field plus pros plus staff). Lead time is 8 to 10 weeks from approved brief. The piece is event-specific, often dated discreetly on the inside.

For the pro shop

We supply pro shops with a retail-tier kit on consignment or net-30 wholesale terms. The kit comes pre-branded with the club crest; per-piece personalisation (initials) is offered at the counter as an upcharge with a 7-to-14-day fulfillment from our atelier. The retail margin sits at industry-standard, and the leather is the same full grain calfskin used in our direct-to-consumer line.
