Luxury Leather Goods, Delivered to Dubai

Goldy Brown ships full grain calfskin holsters, travel bags, and bespoke commissions free of charge across Dubai and the wider UAE. Designed in Vancouver, crafted by master artisans in South India whose tannery houses have supplied European luxury for over a century, delivered to Downtown Dubai, DIFC, and Palm Jumeirah in three to five business days via DHL.

Built for DXB and the long-haul that follows

The Holster is a body-worn travel bag — built for the Dubai client who moves between DIFC meetings, DXB Terminal 3, and the long-haul out to London, Singapore, or Los Angeles in the same week. Pouch fits passport, phone, AirPods, and a slim card holder. Worn over a thobe, under a tropical wool blazer, or with a Brunello tee for the boat — it sits cleanly and rides through security as a worn accessory.

Clients across Downtown Dubai, DIFC, Emirates Hills, Jumeirah, the Palm, Dubai Hills, and the Marina use theirs daily. The full grain calfskin handles desert climate well; we recommend conditioning twice a year given the dryness.
