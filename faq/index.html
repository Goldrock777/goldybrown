Travel and airport security

Has the Goldy Brown Holster been through airport security?

Yes. In our travel testing across major North American airports, the Holster passes through standard security without issue. The hardware is stainless steel of the kind that moves through detectors in millions of belts, watches, and handbags every day. TSA does not formally approve specific products, so we do not claim a designation, but the practical experience is uneventful.

Will the Holster count as a third bag?

The Holster is worn on the body — the same category as a money belt, fanny pack, or sling. Gate agents typically do not count it as a separate item against carry-on or personal item allowances. Airline policy ultimately rests with the agent at the gate, but worn accessories are typically not flagged.

For the deeper version of this answer, see our piece on what counts as a personal item on a flight.

Does the Holster set off metal detectors?

No. The fittings are stainless steel of the same kind found in millions of watches, belts, and handbags that pass through airport scanners daily.

Is the Holster TSA-approved?

TSA does not formally approve specific consumer products. What we can say from regular travel: the Holster passes through TSA, CATSA, and major international security lanes without issue. See our guide to wearing a body-worn bag through security.

Sizing and fit

Will the Holster fit my phone?

Yes. Each pouch is 7" x 5", sized to fit phones up to iPhone 17 Pro Max with a case. AirPods, passport, cards, sunglasses, and a slim wallet also fit comfortably.

Is one size truly one size?

Yes. Adjustable shoulder straps fit chest sizes from approximately 32" to 50". Worn over a shirt, under a jacket, or however you choose.

How do I wear it?

Three working configurations: over a base layer and under a jacket (the cleanest line, recommended for travel); over a shirt with no jacket (the summer configuration); over a jacket (avoid — the strap rides over the shoulder seam awkwardly).

Leather and origin

What kind of leather does Goldy Brown use?

Full grain calfskin from artisan tannery houses of South India — a region whose leather has supplied European luxury houses for over a century. We use vegetable tanning (wattle, myrobalan, and avaram bark, weeks in stone vats and sun-dried) for structured pieces, and chrome tanning for softer goods. Method chosen per piece, never shortcut.

For the long version, see our guide to full grain leather and our comparison of vegetable and chrome tanning.

Where are Goldy Brown bags made?

Designed in Vancouver, British Columbia, and made in South India by master leather artisans whose craft has been handed down across generations.

The longer version: our essay on South Indian leather and the luxury houses.

Why South India?

Three reasons: the climate (vegetable tanning needs sun and air, both abundant in Tamil Nadu), the cattle (small, tight-grained hides ideal for accessories), and the inherited craft (most artisans we work with are third- or fourth-generation in the same trade).

Warranty and care

What does the Goldy Brown lifetime warranty cover?

Any defect in stitching, leather, or hardware for life. We repair or replace the piece at our atelier and cover shipping both ways. No proof of purchase chases, no fine print. A handwritten guarantee card ships with every order.

The warranty does not cover normal wear (scratches, patina, edge softening), abuse (deliberate damage, leaving in a car in summer), or modification by a third-party cobbler.

How do I care for the leather?

Wipe with a dry soft cloth after use. Condition with a neutral leather balm once or twice a year. Avoid prolonged direct sunlight and saltwater. Full grain calfskin softens and deepens in colour with use — the patina is the point.

Full guide: how to care for full grain leather.

What if my piece is damaged?

Email info@goldybrown.com with two photos — one of the issue and one of the whole piece. We respond within 48 hours with a triage and a return label if needed. Most issues are covered under the lifetime warranty.

Personalisation

Can I personalise the leather?

Yes. Hot-stamped initials are included with every piece. Hand-tattooed designs — names, crests, signatures, sketches, club arms — start at $50 USD on top of the piece price. Complex designs are quoted individually.

Can I tattoo a design into the leather?

Yes. Any design can be hand-tattooed into the front face of the holster bag. Email info@goldybrown.com or DM @goldybrowncouture with a reference image before ordering.

How long does personalisation take?

Hot-stamped initials add no extra time to the standard 14-to-28-day handling window. Hand-tattooed designs add approximately one week.

Hotel Programme

Does Goldy Brown make custom leather goods for hotels and clubs?

Yes. The Hotel Programme curates VIP guest kits for 4- and 5-star hotels with budget tiers from $10K to $100K, and we produce branded leather accessories for golf, country, tennis, and racquet clubs. See the Hotel Programme.

How long does a hotel programme take?

Six to ten weeks from approved brief to delivery. Two weeks for design and physical samples, six to eight weeks for production and air-freight shipping. Rush programmes for property openings can be quoted at four weeks.

Do you offer vendor or trade pricing?

Yes. We are registered or in onboarding with Avendra, Hilton Supply Management, Marriott SupplierOne, IHG Procurement, Astria, and SLX Hospitality. Trade pricing is available for property programmes and at-volume orders.

What's the minimum order for the Hotel Programme?

Programme One starts at $10,000 USD and produces approximately 100 to 200 finished pieces depending on configuration. Volume discounts begin at 250 pieces.

Orders, shipping, returns

Does Goldy Brown ship worldwide?

Yes. Free worldwide shipping via DHL, FedEx, and Canada Post — including New York, Los Angeles, Miami, Toronto, London, Milan, Paris, Dubai, and beyond. Typical transit time is 3 to 7 business days after handling. See cities served.

How long does shipping take?

Handling is typically 14 to 28 days for ready-to-wear pieces (we make to order rather than holding inventory). Transit is 3 to 7 business days via DHL, FedEx, or Canada Post depending on destination. Hand-tattoo personalisation adds about a week. Bespoke commissions ship in 4 to 6 weeks.

Are returns and exchanges accepted?

Returns accepted within 14 days of delivery for unused items in original packaging. Hotel Programme and bulk orders are custom-branded and non-returnable, but quality issues are covered under the lifetime warranty.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Shop Pay with Shop Pay Installments. Hotel Programme orders ship on net-30 invoice on approved credit.
