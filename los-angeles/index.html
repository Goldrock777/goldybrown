Goldy Brown ships full grain calfskin holsters, travel bags, and bespoke commissions free of charge across the greater Los Angeles area. Built for LAX, the PCH, and the canyon drive

The Holster is a body-worn travel bag — the answer to the LA traveller's question of how to get from a meeting in Brentwood to a flight at LAX without a backpack and without forgetting the passport at home. Pouch fits passport, phone, AirPods, sunglasses, and a slim wallet. Worn under a linen jacket or over a Buck Mason tee, it disappears in the cabin and reappears at Tom Bradley.

Clients across Beverly Hills, Bel-Air, Brentwood, the Palisades, West Hollywood, Silver Lake, and Manhattan Beach use theirs daily. The leather darkens with sun and salt; we recommend conditioning twice a year if you live coast-side.
