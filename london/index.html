Built for the Heathrow shuttle and the Eurostar

The Holster is a body-worn travel bag — built for the London traveller who moves between Mayfair lunches, Heathrow Terminal 5, the Eurostar at St Pancras, and a Friday flight to Geneva or Milan. Pouch fits passport, phone, AirPods, and a slim wallet. Worn over a Drake's chambray or under an Anderson & Sheppard sport coat, it sits cleanly and rides through security as a worn accessory rather than a third bag.

Clients across Mayfair, Belgravia, Knightsbridge, Chelsea, Marylebone, Holland Park, and Notting Hill use theirs daily. The full grain calfskin handles London weather and softens through a London winter.
