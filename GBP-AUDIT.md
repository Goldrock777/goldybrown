## 4. ATTRIBUTES — toggle every one that applies

GBP attributes show as filter chips and badges. Toggle:

- **From the business** → Identifies as women-owned / family-owned / minority-owned (whichever applies)
- **Service options** → Online estimates · Online appointments · Delivery · In-store pickup *(set to "No" if no retail location)*
- **Accessibility** → only if you take walk-ins
- **Amenities** → Wi-Fi, restroom *(only if you take appointments)*
- **Crowd** → LGBTQ+ friendly, Transgender safespace
- **Planning** → Appointment required · Quick visit
- **Payments** → Credit cards · Debit cards · NFC mobile payments · Apple Pay · Google Pay · Shop Pay (you already accept this)
- **Offerings** → Custom orders · Bespoke service · Gift wrapping · Lifetime warranty *(use the closest available)*
- **Highlights** → Locally owned · Made in-house
- **Recycling** → if you take back/repair under warranty

## 9. REVIEW STRATEGY

### Goal
Match or beat the median competitor on **review velocity** (new reviews per month). Even doubling theirs is achievable — most are at 0–2/mo.

### Get reviews
1. Add a **"Leave a review" link** to the order shipping email and the warranty card. Use the GBP short link from your dashboard (`g.page/r/...`).
2. After every Hotel Programme delivery, ask the procurement contact for a Google review — those reviews carry weight because they mention specific properties.
3. For local Vancouver buyers who pick up, hand them the warranty card with the QR code to the review link.

### Owner-response templates (respond to 100% of reviews within 24h)

**5★ response:**
> Thank you, [Name] — we're glad the [holster / passport holder / kit] is working for you. The full grain calfskin will deepen with every trip. If anything ever needs attention, the lifetime warranty has you covered.
> — The Goldy Brown atelier, Vancouver

**4★ response:**
> Thank you for the honest note, [Name]. We're glad you're enjoying the [piece], and your feedback on [issue] is noted — we read every review at the bench. Email info@goldybrown.com if you'd like us to take a look at it under warranty.

**3★ or below:**
> [Name], thank you for telling us. This isn't the experience we want for any client. We've sent you a note at [email pattern] to make it right — replacement, repair, or refund, your call. The lifetime warranty stands regardless.
> — Shawn, Goldy Brown

(Always sign with a real name on negative reviews. Always work in 1–2 keywords naturally.)
