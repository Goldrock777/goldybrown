Why we exist

The leather goods industry has two honest facts that don't usually appear in marketing copy. The first is that most "Made in France" or "Made in Italy" leather started its life as a hide tanned somewhere else — Tamil Nadu, Vietnam, Brazil — and was finished and stamped in Europe at the last step. The second is that most luxury leather goods today carry the markup of bench-made craft and the construction of factory output.

Goldy Brown was founded on the conviction that you can do better than both. We design in Vancouver, we make in South India where the leather is actually tanned, and we sell direct without the four to five resellers between an Italian atelier and a department store floor. The result is leather goods at $249 that compare to pieces at $1,200, and a brand that is honest about how that price arithmetic works.

Designed in Vancouver. Made in South India. Backed for life.

The atelier

Our Vancouver studio is appointment-only. It is a working atelier — a cutting bench, a sewing bench, a finishing bench, sample books, and the wall of leather hides we work from. We design, prototype, and final-inspect every piece here before it ships. Visitors are welcome by email.

Production happens at three small workshops in Tamil Nadu, where we work with master artisans whose families have tanned and stitched leather for three or four generations. The workshops we partner with are small (10 to 30 people each), pay above the regional craft scale, and run a single shift. The leather is tanned a few miles from where it is cut and sewn — meaning fewer handoffs, less shipping, and a tighter quality loop than the typical European supply chain.

The leather

Full grain calfskin from artisan tannery houses in Tamil Nadu and Kerala. We use vegetable tanning — wattle, myrobalan, avaram bark, weeks in stone vats and sun-dried — for structured pieces like the holster body, briefcases, and document folios. Chrome tanning for soft components: linings, strap padding, anything that needs to drape rather than stand. Method chosen per piece, never shortcut.

The leather we use has, in many cases, also fed European luxury houses for over a century. The tannery houses we work with have supplied finishers in France, Italy, and Switzerland for generations. Our pieces don't make a different leather; they make a shorter trip from skin to bag.

The signature piece

The Goldy Brown Holster is a body-worn leather travel bag. Full grain calfskin, stainless steel hardware, YKK zippers. Sized — pouch 7" × 5" — to fit a passport, an iPhone 17 Pro Max with a case, AirPods, sunglasses, and a slim wallet. Worn under a jacket or over a shell, it pairs with your carry-on as a worn accessory rather than a third bag. $249.

Beyond the holster

We make passport holders, card holders, luggage tags, document folios, garment holders, briefcases, and bespoke commissions. We hot-stamp initials and hand-tattoo crests, signatures, and freehand designs into the leather. We run a Hotel Programme that builds custom-branded VIP guest amenity kits for 4- and 5-star hotels worldwide.
