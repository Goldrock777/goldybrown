Luxury Leather Bags, Delivered to Toronto

Goldy Brown ships full grain calfskin holsters, travel bags, passport holders, and bespoke commissions free of charge across Toronto and the Greater Toronto Area. Designed in Vancouver, crafted by master artisans in South India, delivered to your door via Canada Post Xpresspost in two to four business days.

Made for the Pearson commuter and the Bay Street redeye

The Holster was built for the way Toronto travellers actually move — UP Express to Pearson Terminal 1, Porter out of Billy Bishop, the GO from Union, and the Eglinton Crosstown when it finally runs. Worn under a Mackage shell or over a Canada Goose puffer, it holds your passport, NEXUS card, phone, and a slim wallet without occupying a hand or counting as a third bag. The full grain calfskin handles a Toronto winter and softens through a Toronto spring.

Clients across Yorkville, King West, the Annex, Forest Hill, Rosedale, and Leaside use theirs daily. The leather darkens with PATH humidity and CN Tower wind in equal measure.
