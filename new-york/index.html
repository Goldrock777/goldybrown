Luxury Leather Bags, Delivered to New York

Goldy Brown ships full grain calfskin holsters, travel bags, and bespoke leather goods free of charge to every borough of New York City. Designed in Vancouver, made in South India by master artisans, delivered to your Manhattan door in three to five business days via DHL or FedEx.

Built for the New York commute and the Friday redeye

The Holster is a body-worn travel bag — the answer to the New Yorker's recurring problem of moving between subway, taxi, airport, and meeting without a backpack on your back or a laptop bag in your hand. It holds your passport, phone, AirPods, a slim wallet, and a Moleskine. Worn under a jacket, it disappears at the office; worn over a shell, it rides through TSA and JFK Terminal 4 without an extra-bag conversation.

Clients across Tribeca, SoHo, the West Village, Hudson Yards, and the Upper East Side wear theirs through Penn Station to LIRR, on the JetBlue 6 a.m. to LAX, and on the Eurostar out of St Pancras when work pulls them to London. The leather darkens with use; the patina from a year of New York rides is part of the point.
